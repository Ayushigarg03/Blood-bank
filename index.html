About Blood Donation

Learn about the importance of blood donation

Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a voluntary procedure that can help save lives. Every donation can help up to three people. Blood is needed for emergencies, surgeries, cancer treatment, chronic illnesses, and more.

Donation Requirements

  • Age: 18-65 years old
  • Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Good general health
  • No recent illness or medication
  • No recent tattoos or piercings (within 6 months)

Benefits of Donating

  • Save up to 3 lives with one donation
  • Free health screening
  • Reduced risk of heart disease
  • Burns calories (approximately 650 calories)
  • Sense of civic duty and helping others
